Searching

Clients and Animals

For evette1.1 I have introduced a much more powerful (but more complicated - sorry) search tool for clients and animals:

In previous versions of evette, the only way to find a particular animal was to know the owners details. Using this new search tool it is possible to find an animal even if you do not know who owns it.

Simply fill in details that you know and hit the search button.

Note:
You do NOT need to fill in all of these fields! The fastest way to find a client/animal is to put in the owners surname and/or postcode. If you do not know these, you can attempt to find narrow down the possibilities using the other fields available.

Appointments

Should you want to view all appointments in a certain time frame. Choose Appointment Search from the Appointment menu or to browse appointments on a calendar choose Browse Appointments from the Appointment menu.
