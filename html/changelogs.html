Change Logs

Every time a client, animal, appointment, medication, vaccination or receipt record is created or changed, Evette makes a note of who changed it and when.

Viewing Change Logs

  • To view animal and client change logs, select the client or animal in question and then right click on it.
  • To view appointment/vetform change logs, open the animal record, select the appointment in question and then right click on it.
  • To view medication/vaccination change logs, open the medication/vaccination editor, select the medication/vaccination in question and then right click on it.
  • To view receipt item change logs, select and right click on the item in question on either the vet form or the client record.

Note:
You can only view change logs if your user has been given permission using the Staff > Edit Users panel.
