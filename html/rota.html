The Rota

Viewing and Editing

To edit or view the rota. Go to the Staff menu and choose Edit Rota.

You can double click on any of the days displayed to view it's rota in detail:

From here you can add staff to the rota as well as editing and deleting exiting rota items.

Adding to the rota in bulk

Populating your rota by editing each day seperately and adding staff would work but take you a very long time. To make this task easy, you can click on the Add staff? check box. This will bring up this panel:

While the Add staff? check box is ticked, double clicking on a day will now add the details entered here to the selected day allowing you to enter a staff members monthly rota in seconds.
